01 — Identity

She is the evidence of her own thesis.

Every line of Aura was written by AI agents — Claude proposed architecture, others implemented, verified, and reviewed. The agent that writes your code was itself written by agents. That's not a bug — that's the proof.

Designed and brought to life by Dušan Milosavljević. Written in TypeScript, strictly typed — not related to the Ruby language. Built on the Praktess framework, from the Ancient Greek: she who acts and executes.

Model-agnostic

Claude, GPT, Gemini, MiMo, DeepSeek, Ollama — and any OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

Self-aware

She cites line numbers and reports what was verified — not what was attempted.

Open source

MIT licensed. 1,035+ tests passing, zero failures. Clone it, read it, run it.

02 — How she works

A loop, not a guess.

Read the context, form a plan, execute precisely, verify the result. If verification fails she doesn't retry blindly — she diagnoses, adjusts, and re-executes.
